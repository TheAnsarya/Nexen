### Issue #1229 — Add Sealed Modifier to Non-Inherited C# Classes

Audited the entire C# codebase. The project was already well-sealed — only 2 production classes and 14 benchmark classes needed `sealed`:

- **UI**: `SingleInstance` (also changed `Dispose(bool)` from `protected virtual` to `private`), `ControllerConfigViewLocator`
- **Benchmarks**: All 14 benchmark classes (`ColorConversionBenchmarks`, `CollectionPatternBenchmarks`, `GreenzoneManagerBenchmarks`, `FullscreenResolutionBenchmarks`, `ControllerInputBenchmarks`, `GameDataManagerBenchmarks`, `PansyExportBenchmarks`, `WatchFormatBenchmarks`, `Utf8UtilitiesBenchmarks`, `UiPerformanceBenchmarks`, `TasUiBenchmarks`, `Program`, `TasHotPathBenchmarks`, `TasOptimizationBenchmarks`)

### Issue #1230 — Reduce Lock Contention in GreenzoneManager

Analyzed lock patterns in both `PianoRollControl` and `GreenzoneManager`:

- **PianoRollControl**: Single lock (`_frameNumberCacheLock`) with LRU mutation on every access — `ReaderWriterLockSlim` not beneficial. Left as-is.
- **GreenzoneManager**: Consolidated `_indexLock` and `_ringBufferLock` into single `_stateLock`:
	- Eliminated nested lock in `AddToRingBuffer` (deadlock risk: `_ringBufferLock` → `_indexLock`)
	- Reduced per-frame lock acquisitions during TAS recording
	- Merged sequential lock blocks in `InvalidateFrom` and `Clear`
	- `_seekLock` and `_compressionSemaphore` kept separate (different concerns, low frequency)
