### Nexen — Piano Roll RefreshMarkerEntries Cache (#1392)

- **Issue:** [#1392](https://github.com/TheAnsarya/Nexen/issues/1392) — `RefreshMarkerEntries` scanned ALL frames (O(n)) on every call including filter changes
